"From Gears to Code: A Certifiable Ode"

Shifting from Mechanical Engineering to Computer Science, I embarked on a transformative journey marked by a hunger for knowledge. Online certifications from the University of Michigan in SQL and Python, Duke University in Tableau, Intel OpenVINO for AI, and AWS for cloud computing became my stepping stones. Each certification, not just a badge, but a testament to my commitment to bridging the gap between disciplines. These endeavors represent more than acquiring skills; they shape a narrative of continuous learning, adaptation, and a passion for technology. Now, at the intersection of Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science, these certifications illuminate the path where resilience and knowledge converge, creating a professional poised for success in the digital era.

"Academic Odyssey, Caffeine Chronicles, and Fitness Feats"

In the pages of my academic pursuits, I navigated the intricate waters of science, mathematics, and technology, culminating in a graduation in Mechanical Engineering. Within the gears and cogs of academia, a spark ignited – the allure of data analytics. It was here that the seeds were planted for a flourishing career in this dynamic sector. Fast forward to the bustling world of Tim Hortons and GoodLife Fitness, where I not only served up caffeine-infused delights but also took on the role of a fitness aficionado. Imagine me orchestrating the ballet of coffee cups and engaging in the rigorous routines of customer service at GoodLife Fitness, a feat akin to a tightrope walker donning an invisible circus hat. A 14-hour shift was not just a job; it was a performance, balancing work responsibilities with the pursuit of a master's degree.

"Mastering the Data Dance: A Transition's Chance"

As I approach the completion of my master's degree at Trent University, I am actively seeking opportunities in data science. Armed with skills honed in statistical and mathematical algorithms, I am eager to contribute to a company's success. From juggling roles as a coffee barista and fitness enthusiast at GoodLife Fitness to the academic challenges faced at Trent, I am now poised to transition into a role where I can apply my expertise and make a significant impact. My goal is clear: to contribute meaningfully to a data-driven future, turning aspirations into tangible successes for the company that chooses to harness my capabilities.

What am I eagerly anticipating and diligently working towards in the future?

Secure a position in the realm of data science before my birthday in July.
